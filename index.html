Who We Are

Aurova Interiors is a modern interior design studio delivering premium solutions for both residential and commercial spaces. We specialize in creating stylish, functional, and elegant environments tailored to contemporary lifestyles. From luxurious home interiors to efficient and aesthetic office and commercial designs, our approach blends creativity, space optimization, and modern design trends. We provide complete end-to-end solutions — from concept and 3D visualization to execution — ensuring every space reflects sophistication, comfort, and purpose.

Our Services

Home Interiors

We design modern, luxurious, and highly functional home interiors that reflect your personality while maximizing comfort, aesthetics, and space efficiency.

Office Design

Our commercial designs focus on productivity, brand identity, and modern work culture, creating inspiring office environments that enhance performance.

3D Design

We provide realistic 3D visualizations so you can experience your space before execution, ensuring clarity, precision, and perfect design outcomes.

Renovation

Transform your existing spaces with our renovation services, combining modern design upgrades with structural improvements for a fresh new look.
